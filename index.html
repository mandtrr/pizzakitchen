The Pizza Kitchen

The Pizza Kitchen is one of the most popular in Portugal. Try their pizza, and you'll soon see why.

The Pizza Kitchen has been a Slice partner since 2022. They've been serving the local community for a while now, so you can rely on them providing great pizza. Call ahead and get curbside pickup at The Pizza Kitchen. It's never been easier to get hot, fresh pizza to go.

How to order?

To order your favorite pizza, simply give us a call at our dedicated phone number: 221 224 220. Our friendly staff will be ready to assist you with your order.

We offer delivery services straight to your doorstep, ensuring that you can enjoy our delicious pizzas from the comfort of your own home. Our delivery hours are from 11:00 AM until midnight, seven days a week.

Whether it's lunchtime or a late-night craving, we're here to satisfy your hunger with our freshly made pizzas. Don't hesitate to give us a call and treat yourself to a taste of The Pizza Kitchen today!
